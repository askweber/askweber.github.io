  3. Using your knowledge of the nature of phospholipids, explain what you think would happen if phospholipids are mixed with water. If you are having trouble visualising this, think about what happens when oil is added to water. Can you suggest a physiological process where this might be important?

    • 1.3. Canvas solutions

  5. Channelopathies are rare genetic or acquired diseases that affect the cell membrane channels. In broad terms, what effects do you think channelopathies have (i.e. what symptoms would they produce)?

  7. How does the role of cell membrane carriers differ from that of channels?
