In 2019 Eliud Kipchoge became the first person to run a marathon on in under 2 hours. He had begun his career as a 5000 meter runner, but like many middle-distance athletes, successfully converted to the longer event. In contrast, it is unheard of for an elite sprinter to become an elite marathoner.
